The Artist

Who Is Arpit?

Arpit Bala (born Arpit Balabantaray, May 20, 1999) is an Indian rapper, lyricist and YouTube creator from Faridabad, Haryana. With 4.6M monthly Spotify listeners, he is one of the most compelling voices in Indian indie hip-hop.

Known for sharp humor, emotional depth, and desi storytelling, Arpit fuses ancient Indian melodies with contemporary hip-hop beats to create something wholly unique — from love and longing to sharp social commentary.

From college sketches to national stages, backed by Gully Gang Records & Sony Music India, Arpit continues to redefine what Indian hip-hop can sound like.
